\f0\fs24 \cf0 ## Client AI Lead Engine\
\
### Overview\
Production-ready AI system designed to automate lead qualification\
and prioritization for client sales teams.\
\
### Business Problem\
Sales teams lose time and revenue due to manual lead handling,\
poor qualification, and slow response times.\
\
### Solution Architecture\
An AI-driven decision engine orchestrated via Make.com,\
using Airtable as the structured data layer.\
\
### AI Logic\
- Lead intent detection\
- Budget and urgency signal extraction\
- AI-based lead scoring\
- Priority classification (Hot / Warm / Cold)\
\
### Workflow\
1. Lead submission via webhook or form\
2. AI analyzes lead context and intent\
3. Lead score and priority assigned\
4. CRM updated automatically\
5. Sales notifications triggered\
\
### Tech Stack\
- Make.com (orchestration)\
- Airtable (data layer)\
- OpenAI / LLMs (decision logic)\
- Webhooks & APIs\
\
### Business Impact\
- Faster response times\
- Higher lead quality\
- Reduced manual workload\
- Increased conversion rates\
